# METRICS

| Metric | Definition | Early Target | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Proposal packs generated | Number of complete proposal packs created | 10 in first month | Measures real workflow usage |
| Time to first value | Time from signup to first usable output | < 15 minutes | Strong activation indicator |
| Repeat usage | Users who run a second brief | 50% of activated users | Signals workflow fit |
| Paid conversion | Users who pay after concierge/sample use | 20% of qualified users | Revenue proof |
| Claimed time saved | User-reported hours saved per proposal | 2+ hours | Clear ROI story |

## North-Star
Qualified proposal packs generated for real deals.
