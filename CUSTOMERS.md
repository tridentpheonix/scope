# CUSTOMERS

## Ideal Customer Profile
- 1-20 person agency or freelancer
- sells defined services but still scopes manually
- regularly writes proposals and SOWs
- feels scope creep, underpricing, or proposal fatigue

## First 10 Target User Types
- freelance web designers
- boutique branding studios
- small marketing agencies
- dev shops selling fixed-scope builds

## First Conversation Goal
Learn:
- how they scope today
- where briefs are usually incomplete
- what causes underpricing
- what makes them reject AI-generated commercial docs

## Interview Questions
1. Walk me through your last messy brief.
2. Where do you lose the most time in proposal creation?
3. What usually causes scope creep after signing?
4. Would you trust a tool to draft exclusions and assumptions? Why or why not?
5. What would make this immediately worth paying for?
